Pulau Pinang - Botanic Gardens, Jalan Air Terjun

This 30-hectare sprawling garden of flora and fauna is a favourite recreation spot for locals. Central to the Gardens is a waterfall that start over a hundred metres above, hence its more popular moniker of "Waterfall Gardens". Today, the waterfall is a restricted area and hidden from public view by the green foliage. For more information on Penang Botanic Gardens, visit its official website Address: Jalan Kebun Bunga, 10350 Pulau Pinang, Malaysia Tel: +6 04 2270 428, 2271 354 (direct line) Opens: Daily from 5.00am - 8.00pm Admission is free.
